## What it is

Shikki orchestrates AI engineering work the way a general contractor runs a build site — multiple specialists working in parallel under a single coherent plan, with the developer in the architect seat.

- **Parallel agents** with mesh coordination (NATS), structured memory (pgvector + BM25), and per-task fitness scoring
- **Swift + Go** core. AGPL-3.0
- **Self-hosted-first** — your code, your infra, your data sovereignty
- **Built by** [Jeoffrey Thirot](https://obyw.one) at OBYW.one
